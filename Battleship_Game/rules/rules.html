Battleship Game Rules

    • Each player gets two 10*10 grids—one for their fleet, one to track enemy attacks.
    • Before battle, secretly place your ships on your grid. Keep their locations a mystery!
    • Take turns calling out coordinates to fire shots at your opponent's grid.
    • Hit a ship? Mark it! Miss? Keep hunting! Use red pegs for hits and white for misses.
    • First player to sink all enemy ships wins the war.
    • Ready to command your fleet?
