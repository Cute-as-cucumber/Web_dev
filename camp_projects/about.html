ABOUT

Follow the dawn of artificial consciousness and the evolution of sin in this dark odyssey that begins in a world where every human appetite can be indulged.

The luxury resort, imagined. Westworld channels your inner desires--be they rest or rebellion--into a transportive theme park experience. Our immerse worlds integrate inspired technology, provocative narriatives, and unprecedented innovation to offer an oppotunity that redefines life itself: the chance to change your story.

In a world that's become inundated by the virtual, escape the secreen and take hold of a freedom you can physically touch. It's a feeling that will change your idea of reality-- and as our friends here like to say, if you can't tell the difference, does it matter?
